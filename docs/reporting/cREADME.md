# Reporting

## RAID Management
- **Risks:** Potential future events with probability/impact.
- **Assumptions:** Beliefs that must be validated.
- **Issues:** Current problems requiring action.
- **Dependencies:** External items impacting delivery.

**Guidance**
- Review RAID weekly.
- Assign owners and due dates for mitigations.
- Escalate high-impact/high-probability risks.

## Burn-Up / Burn-Down
- **Burn-up:** Track completed scope vs total scope.
- **Burn-down:** Track remaining work within sprint.

**Usage**
- Update charts at least weekly.
- Use scope change annotations to maintain transparency.

## Gate Status Reporting
Report gate readiness with a consistent status model.

**Status Definitions**
- **Green:** All entry/exit criteria met.
- **Yellow:** Minor gaps with mitigation plans.
- **Red:** Major gaps or blockers.

**Recommended Report Fields**
- Phase/Gate target date.
- Current status (Green/Yellow/Red).
- Top 3 risks and mitigations.
- Key decisions pending.
- Evidence readiness checklist completion %.

## Reporting Cadence
- **Weekly status report:** Summary, risks, metrics.
- **Per-gate report:** Gate pack with evidence.
- **Executive summary:** Monthly high-level status and outcomes.
